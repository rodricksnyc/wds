What is this?

Overview

A Design System is the source which groups all the elements that will allow the teams to design, realize and develop a product. A good design system is:

  • Reusable
    A design system is a series of components that can be reused in different combinations. A Design System is not a deliverable, but a set of deliverables.
  • Manageable
    Design systems allow you to manage design at scale. A team can zero in on the components that they need most.
  • Ever-Evolving
    It will evolve constantly with the product, the tools and the new technologies. It can stand the test of time and grow to suit the needs of design and development teams.
Styles come and go. Good design is a language, not a style.

-Massimo Vignelli

Benefits

What value does it provide?

  • Increases Efficiency
    Using a design system reduces cost and decreases deployment time. By having pre-defined standards and components available to anyone at Westat, project teams can focus on developing a high-quality end product.
  • Sets Organization-Wide Standards
    A good design system gives the company a recognizable identity and an exceptional standard of quality that our clients can expect of Westat. A strong brand can unify a company and all of its products and work.
  • Encourages Teamwork
    Having a design system allows Westat teams to hone in on a single vision and narrow their project and group goals. It helps facilitate their work and optimze operation for projects.

How it Works

The Design system has compiled design elements, guidelines, components, UI kits, and more in an organized and searchable way. You can search directly for the component you need, or browse Guidelines, UI Kits, or Component Libraries to find what suits your project needs. You will be able to get the code or the elements themselves right from this design system site.
